Young Twig lives in the Deepwoods, among the Woodtrolls, but he isn't one of them. In a brave attempt to find where he belongs, Twig wanders into the mysterious world beyond the Deepwoods. He meets a collection of odd companions, such as his wise guardian, the Caterbird; the Slaughterers, a peaceful race who butcher animals; and the vicious, bile-swilling Rotsucker. Twig steadfastly pursues his quest until he discovers his roots, not among the trees, but in the skies…
