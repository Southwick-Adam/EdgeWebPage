Places

The Deep Woods

A huge forest, described as endless. Different species of plants and animals, many of which were predatory or poisonous, make the Deepwoods a very dangerous place, especially at night.

Sanctaphrax

A city-state that rose high above Undertown. Built upon an enormous floating rock, and fastened with massive chains, it was the seat of knowledge and academia in The Edge.

Undertown

The bustling main city of The Edge. It was intended to be a haven for those seeking escape from the oppressive slavery and danger of the Deepwoods.

Farrow Ridge

A small, sparsely-populated but growing settlement in the western Deepwoods. The home of the primitive clan of White Trogs that live in the Stalactite Forests.

Gorge Town

A small city populated predominantly by Quarry Frogs. Buildings were constructed along the cliff-sides above the dark caverns below.

Riverrise

The highest point in The Edge, and the source of the Edgewater River. A sacred place, and the origin of all life in the Edge, considered by most to be a myth.

Creatures

Bandabear

Enormous, hairy, tusked beasts. Despite their ferocious appearance and tremendous strength, Banderbears are shy, timid, noble, and gentle creatures.

Shryke

Nomadic slavers, running the Great slave market and using their immunity to the madness of the twilight woods to monopolize trade on the Edge.

Gloamglozer

The most evil creature in the Edge. A trickster and seducer that was created before the dawn of time, terrorising the Deepwoods.

Twilight Walkers

Men driven mad by the light of the twilight forest. Endlessly wandering, unable to escape or even die.

Logworm

A predator of the Deepwoods Extremely deadly, with the ability to float and inhale anything in their path.

Muglump

Large, deadly creatures found inhabiting the Mire. They often posed a threat to convoys trying to cross the area to reach Undertown.

Spindlebug

A unique species of insect in the Edge. Capable of intelligent thought and speech, they often from alliances with other sentient creatures.

Slime Mole

Huge, transparent creatures with glistening, jelly-like bodies. The mole glue they excrete is required for flight craft production.

Wig Wig

Small, orange, fluffy creatures with terrible ferocity, found in the Deepwoods Feared by all who knew of their temperament and appetite.

Characters

Twig

An Orphan in the Deep woods, Twig would grow to become the most famous Sky Pirate in all of the Edge. Captaining the last known Sky Ship: The SkyRaider.

Tem

Twigs fearless first mate. He accompanied his captain from the dangers of the twilight woods and Riverrise to over the Edge itself.

Mud Bandit

A former Santaphrax knight, this outlaw lurks in the mire, praying on anyone who passes to slowly complete his last mission.

Quint

Twigs father, also a Sky Captain in his time, and the man who first encountered the dreaded Gloamglozer.

Orbix

The leader of the terrible and deadly Guardians of Night. He lives on falling Sanctaphrax making sure it never touches the ground.

Rook

a young Librarian Knight. One of the chosen few to travel to the freeglades and join in the battle again the dreaded Guardians of the Night.

Xanth

A former Guardian who defected to the Librarian Knights to bring an end to his old companions tyrany.

Proffesor of Light

A leader of the Earth Scholars living in the seweres below Undertown. He studies the earth to find a cure to mysterious stone sickness.
